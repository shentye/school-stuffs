Explore Melbourne in a Day - A Guide for Exchange Students!

Welcome, exchange students! This guide will help you pack a day full of Melbourne's vibrant culture, iconic landmarks, and hidden gems. Get ready for a whirlwind adventure!

Morning Itinerary

Breakfast at a Local Cafe

Embrace Melbourne's coffee culture and indulge in a flat white or latte, accompanied by a flaky croissant or a hearty eggs Benedict. Most cafes are located within walking distance of Flinders Street Station.

Royal Botanic Gardens

Immerse yourself in the beauty of the Royal Botanic Gardens. Explore diverse plant collections, stroll through tranquil gardens, or admire the iconic glasshouse.
(Tram #8 from Flinders Street Station, 19 minutes)

Afternoon Itinerary

Lunch at Queen Victoria Market

Experience the bustling atmosphere of Queen Victoria Market, Melbourne's largest open-air market. Sample fresh produce, indulge in international cuisines, or grab a delicious hot jam donut!
(Tram #57 from Collins Street, 15 minutes)

Federation Square & ACMI

Visit Federation Square, Melbourne's vibrant cultural hub, and explore the Australian Centre for the Moving Image (ACMI) for a glimpse into Australian film and television history.
(Tram #19 from Queen Victoria Market, 10 minutes)

Evening Itinerary

Dinner in Lygon Street

Immerse yourself in Melbourne's Italian heritage with a delicious dinner in Lygon Street. Choose from a variety of authentic Italian restaurants, cafes, and gelaterias.
(Tram #86 from Federation Square, 12 minutes)

Eureka Skydeck

Ascend to the top of Eureka Skydeck for breathtaking panoramic views of Melbourne at sunset. Witness the city transform with a dazzling display of twinkling lights.
(Tram #86 from Lygon Street, 12 minutes)
